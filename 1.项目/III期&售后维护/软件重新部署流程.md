## 部署frpc

```
wget http://118.31.36.32:9999/emd/deployment/frp.tar -P /opt/
tar -xvf /opt/frp.tar -C /opt/
macaddress=`cat /sys/class/net/eth0/address`
filename='/opt/frp/frpc.ini'
sed -i "5c [ssh-$macaddress]" $filename
rm -f /opt/frp.tar
```
